Important use notice

GridMap is an unofficial reference tool. The app shell/code may be publicly hosted, but any imported data, saved data, exported files, screenshots, search results, copied information, or work use must only be handled by current authorised users with access to approved systems needed to verify it.

Use and access

Only use GridMap for legitimate work reference while you remain part of the current authorised work group and can verify all information in approved systems. Do not send the app link, imported data, exported files, screenshots, search results, copied files, or use instructions to anyone outside that authorised group.

If access changes

If you no longer need GridMap, leave the work group, change roles, lose access to approved systems, are asked to stop using it, or are no longer authorised to use it, you agree to delete all imported/exported app data, clear saved app data, and uninstall GridMap from every device you control.

Data sources and accuracy

The app may reference publicly available Western Australian location and asset-related datasets and mapping context. Public availability does not mean the information is approved for operational use. Information may be incorrect, incomplete, outdated, duplicated, missing, estimated, or incorrectly mapped.

Do not rely on this app for operational decisions

Do not use GridMap or Google Maps links as a source of truth for safety, switching, isolation, access, construction, design, asset verification, navigation, route selection, work planning, driving, vehicle/plant operation, or any operational decision.

GridMap is not approved, verified, or endorsed by any employer, network operator, data provider, mapping provider, or official system owner. Always verify information against approved internal systems, current work packs, official asset records, permits, procedures, and actual site conditions before any action or work is undertaken. Use of GridMap is entirely at your own risk.
